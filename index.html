📸 🎥 About Media Clusterer

A high-performance, private-by-design media management tool. Everything happens locally on your device—no images or videos are ever uploaded to a server.

🧠 How it Works

The app uses WebGPU and Transformers.js to run AI models directly in your browser:

  • Nomic-Embed-Vision: Generates 768-dimensional vectors for every image and video frame.
  • UMAP: Projects these high-dimensional vectors into the 2D map you see on the canvas.
  • Semantic Search: Uses Nomic-Embed-Text to let you find media by natural language descriptions.

🛡️ Privacy Policy

Zero tracking. Zero uploads. Your local folder stays local. Even the AI models are cached in your browser's IndexedDB for offline use.

🖱️ Navigation

  • Pan: Click and drag the canvas.
  • Zoom: Mouse wheel or pinch gesture.
  • View: Tap any media to see it full-size.
  • Search: Type a description to highlight matching media.
